What makes CHOAS VISULIZATION different?

At CHOAS VISULIZATION, we take a scientific approach to advertising. Just as the term CHOAS VISULIZATION suggests, we aim to create marketing campaigns that stick with your target audience, resulting in maximum engagement and return of investment (ROI). Leveraging data and technology, we deliver strategies tailored to your unique brand, ensuring impactful and measurable results.

What is your pricing model or how do you charge for services?

At CHOAS VISULIZATION, our pricing model is primarily performance-based. We strongly believe in creating value for our clients, and this is reflected in how we charge for our services. Rather than a flat fee, our compensation is usually tied to the results we deliver—be it sales or leads. In essence, we succeed when you do. This performance-driven approach ensures our goals align with yours and fosters a partnership rooted in shared success.

How does the billing process work for your digital marketing services, and do I need to sign a contract?

The billing process begins once the terms of the contract are agreed upon. A formal contract is typically required to ensure both parties are clear on the services, deliverables, and payment terms. Invoices are usually issued monthly, but this can be adapted based on the project's needs.

How does CHOAS VISULIZATION generate leads/sales for clients?

At CHOAS VISULIZATION, our lead and sales generation strategies are built upon a blend of proven advertising platforms. From native advertising and social media to the expansive reach of Google's network, we create multi-channel campaigns tailored to engage your specific target audience effectively. By leveraging data insights and audience behavior, we craft strategies that not only attract potential customers but also drive them to action, resulting in quality leads and sales that align with your business objectives. Our aim is to ensure every marketing dollar spent translates into tangible business growth

How does CHOAS VISULIZATION track and manage leads and sales? What integration options do you offer?

At CHOAS VISULIZATION, we use sophisticated tracking tools like Voluum and Everflow, supplemented by our custom in-house technology, to follow the journey of each visitor from the first point of engagement to conversion into a lead or sale. This approach allows us to capture every interaction with your website and campaigns, providing a rich tapestry of data that offers valuable insights into customer behavior and campaign effectiveness. Additionally, our systems can integrate smoothly with a variety of CRMs and marketing platforms, creating a unified data flow for accurate tracking, efficient management, and optimal adjustment of your leads and sales.

In which markets/countries does CHOAS VISULIZATION operate?
